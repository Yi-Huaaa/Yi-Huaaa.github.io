About me

Infomation

I’m Yi-Hua Chung, an undergraduate at Graduate Institute of Networking and Multimedia of National Taiwan University (GINM, NTU).
Now, I pursue the M.S. degree instructed by Prof. Shih-Hao Hung in Performance, Applications, and Security Lab (PAS Lab).
Our team has worked in the field of simulation of quantum annealing, like simulated quantum annealing (SQA), simulated bifurcation (SB). Since annealing is a slow process, we are trying to use different accelerators, like FPGAs, GPUs, and Tensor cores (architecture on GPU), to improve the speed. Also, using some skills to improve the performance of the SQA.
The research paper about SQA accelerated by GPU and Tensor cores has been accepted by ISC high performance 2022.
Recently, we focus on building up the circuit-based quantum simulator by using some computer architecture skills (e.g. AVX, cache blocking, …, etc.) to accelerate the simulation of some deeper quantum circuits.

Research Fields

  • Quantum based: Simulated Quantum Annealing, Quantum Circuit Simulation, Coherence Ising Machine
  • Computer science based: Accelerators, Simulation, Parallel computing, GPU, Tensor Cores.
