About me

Infomation

I’m Yi-Hua Chung, an undergraduate at Graduate Institute of Networking and Multimedia of National Taiwan University at National Taiwan University (GINM, NTU).
Now, I pursue the M.S. degree instructed by Prof. Shih-Hao Hung in Performance, Applications, and Security Lab (PAS Lab).
Currently, our team works in the field of simulation of quantum annealing, like simulated quantum annealing (SQA), simulated bifurcation (SB). Since annealing is a slow process, we are trying to use different accelerators, like FPGAs, GPUs, and Tensor cores (architecture on GPU), to improve the speed. Also, using some skills to improve the performance of the SQA, like reinforcement learning.
