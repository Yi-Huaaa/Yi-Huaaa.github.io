About me

Infomation

I’m a research assistant at the Institute of Information Science, Academia Sinica, advised by Prof. Bo-Yin Yang.
I pursued my M.S. degree instructed by Prof. Shih-Hao Hung in Performance, Applications, and Security Lab (PAS Lab) from 2021.01 to 2022.08.
During my master’s degree study, I focused on constructing a large-scale, cost-effective, and high-performance quantum circuit simulator, accelerating by adopting some computer architecture skills (e.g., OpenMP, AVX, cache blocking…, etc.).
Besides, I proposed the quantum circuit scheduler in my thesis to simply the input quantum circuit depth by integrating the quantum gate to get more acceleration, which allows our SSD-based simulator to become faster than the RAM-based simulator.
Furthermore, we also worked in the field of simulated quantum annealing (SQA). Since the annealing step is slow, we used GPUs and Tensor cores (architecture on GPU) to make some speedup. The research paper about SQA accelerated by GPU and Tensor cores was published in ISC high performance 2022.
