Information

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at UW-Madison, advised by Prof. Tsung-Wei (TW) Huang. My research focuses on high-performance computing, parallel computing, and GPU acceleration for design automation problems.

Currently, I am particularly interested in developing GPU-friendly partitioning algorithms to accelerate logic simulation on GPU and exploring hybrid CPU-GPU computing to optimize the runtime of gate-sizing on GPU.
